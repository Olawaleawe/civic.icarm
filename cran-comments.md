## Notes
civic.icarm provides a general-purpose framework for interpretable,
civic-accountable and responsible machine learning (ICARM). It works
with any clean tabular data, auto-detects binary classification,
multi-class classification, or regression from the target variable,
and provides fairness auditing, calibration, audit trails, and
accountability scorecards. Developed under the DataCitizen-Pro
DFG Sachbeihilfe research programme at Ludwigsburg University
of Education, Germany.
